Blog Post 1 Concept Design Build

Posted by Susan Sewe on 1st November 2023

At Silchip we believe in providing comprehensive solutions for any engineering challenge or problem. We partner and help our clients conceptualise their ideas, we then create a workable design for each concept and finally build a long-lasting solution. Concept Concepts are abstract ideas we have in our heads. This is the first stage of our solutions development cycle. In this stage we endeavour to answer the following questions: 1. Who is this client and what is the nature of his business? 2. What is the challenge or problem? 3. Has the challenge been solved before? 4. Is it a re-occurring challenge? 5. What’s the solution from the client’s perspective? The end product of this stage is a clear definition of the problem/challenge and a mental image of how the solution will look like.
